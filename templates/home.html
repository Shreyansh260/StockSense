How it works

01
Select a Stock

Pick from US or Indian market stocks using the visual card selector.

02
Fetch News

Latest articles are pulled from NewsAPI and full content is scraped.

03
Run DistilBERT

Each article is chunked and analyzed for positive, negative, or neutral sentiment.

04
Technical Analysis

RSI, MACD and moving averages are computed from live price data.

05
Get Recommendation

A BUY, SELL, or HOLD signal is generated when both signals agree.

About

Built with cutting-edge AI

StockSense AI is an intelligent financial analysis platform that combines Natural Language Processing with classical technical analysis to give traders and investors a data-driven edge.

Rather than relying on a single signal, StockSense uses a confirmation approach — a recommendation is only issued when both the news sentiment and the chart technicals agree, significantly reducing false positives.
