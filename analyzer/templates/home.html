About the Project

AI Stock Sentiment Analyzer is an intelligent financial analysis platform that uses Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning to understand market sentiment from financial news sources. By combining stock market data with AI-driven sentiment analysis, the system helps users understand whether the market perception of a stock is positive, negative, or neutral.

Built with Django • HuggingFace Transformers • Chart.js
